Why Video Games?

For me, video games are more than just a pastime. They are interactive art forms that can tell profound stories, challenge our problem-solving skills, connect us with friends, and allow us to explore worlds beyond our imagination. From the thrill of a difficult boss battle to the satisfaction of a well-executed strategy, games offer a unique blend of engagement and escapism.
